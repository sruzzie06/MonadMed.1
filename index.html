What is MediVault?

MediVault Lite is a decentralized application built on the Monad blockchain that allows you to securely store and retrieve critical emergency medical information. In case of an emergency, first responders can quickly access your vital medical data including blood group, emergency contacts, and medical notes.

Your data is encrypted and stored on the blockchain, ensuring it's always available when you need it most.

How It Works

  1. Connect Wallet: Sign in with your MetaMask wallet
  2. Store Your Info: Enter your blood group, emergency contact, and medical notes
  3. Save to Blockchain: Your data is securely stored on Monad blockchain
  4. Emergency Access: Your information can be retrieved anytime

Why Blockchain?

  • ✓ Decentralized - No single point of failure
  • ✓ Transparent - You control your data
  • ✓ Immutable - Your records are permanent and secure
  • ✓ Always Available - Access your info from anywhere
